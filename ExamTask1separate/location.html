Locations of the rhino species

This part of the site is there for you to see where the rhinos live and see how far you live away from them. Of course this is only the distance, and rhino sanctuaries are normally rather remote. Nevertheless, hopefully this give people an idea about how far they live.

Just press the first button below to get your current location. Then press the second button to get the distances (with the Haversine formula) to the rhino sanctuaries from your current location.
